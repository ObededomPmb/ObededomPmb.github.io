About Us

Welcome to Obededom Church! We are a community dedicated to sharing the love of Christ and serving our neighbors.

Our History

Founded in 1995, Obededom Church began as a small gathering of believers seeking to make a difference in our community. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant congregation committed to worship, fellowship, and outreach.

Ministry Objectives

  • To foster a welcoming environment for all individuals.
  • To provide spiritual guidance and support through Bible study and prayer.
  • To engage in community service and outreach programs.
  • To nurture the next generation of leaders through mentorship and education.

Mission Statement

We exist to glorify God by making disciples of all nations, teaching them to obey His commands and serving our community with love and compassion.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be a beacon of hope and transformation in our community, where every individual experiences the love of God and the power of the Gospel.
